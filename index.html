01: Product

WiseFlow

Personal finance that finally makes sense.

A solo-built Android personal finance app, live on Google Play. At its center is Wisey, an in-app AI advisor that reads your real spending data and answers money questions in plain language. Around it: expense and income tracking, wallets and budgets, savings goals, bill and debt tracking, a financial health score, and a weekly AI-generated spending digest.

I owned the full lifecycle: product planning, UI/UX, Android development, backend integration, debugging, and a closed beta with 150 testers whose feedback shaped the launch.
